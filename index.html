MAGIC BUS founded by Matthew Spacie in 1999, Magic Bus equips children and young people in the age group of 12 to 18,

with skills and knowledge they need, to grow up and down and move out of poverty. This takes them from a childhood full of challenges to a life with meaningful livelihoods.

There are now 5.500 young leaders , from the community who have been deliver the sports activity based sessions to children across 930 schools. Magic Bus also works in Nepal, Bangladesh and Myanmar

Since its inception, Magic Bus has transformed the lives of one million children and young people helping them move out poverty
